Jules Simplicio designs and develops software. They are currently helping build tools and product experiences at Sprout Social.

Product Strategy

I help businesses focus on a vision by defining goals. I help build a roadmap and measure success so products can achieve their goals and provide the most value to users.

Wireframing and Prototyping

I design wireframes and use them to prototype solutions to user needs that are used for user testing in order to validate any assumptions.

User Experience Research

I improve products usability and usefulness by empathizing with users and by understanding their behaviors. I use a variety of methods to gather qualitative and quantitative data and insights.

Web and Desktop App Design

I design the interface and experience of products. I create improved experiences focusing on functionality and usability. I componetize product interfaces to help build and maintain design systems.

Front-End Software Development

I prototype designs in code during ideating and testing. I translate final designs into production code with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I use modern technologies and frameworks including Scss, and React. I help develop components and document design systems.

Mobile App Design

I design mobile experiences and interfaces for native Android and iOS, and React Native apps.
