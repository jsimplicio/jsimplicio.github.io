Jules Simplicio designs and develops software. They are currently helping build tools and product experiences at Sprout Social.

Product Strategy

I help businesses stand out by focusing on a vision and defining goals. I help build a roadmap so products can achieve set goals in order to provide value to customers by giving them what they need, and measuring success.

User Experience Research

I improve a product's usability and usefulness by empathizing with current and potential customers, and by understanding their behaviors. I use a variety of methods to gather qualitative and quantitative data and insights.

Wireframing and Prototyping

Based on research results and user feedback, I work on initial wireframe designs and prototypes based on the pre-defined user flows, that are used for testing to inform interaction patterns for the defined problems at hand.

Web and Desktop App Design

I design the interface and experience of products, aligned with product goals. I create innovative and improved experiences, with better functionality and usability. I componetize designs and help build and maintain design systems.

Front-End Software Development

I prototype designs in code during testing. I translate final designs into production ready code utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with modern technologies and frameworks including Scss, and React. I help develop and document design systems.

Mobile App Design

I design mobile experiences for both Android and iOS apps, and also React Native.
