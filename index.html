About

I have worked in software for the past 10 years. I have spent my career at startups, small and medium sized businesses, and mid-market companies. I have focused on web and mobile application design, design systems, and front-end development for education, ecommerce, research, and free and open-source software products.

My pronouns are they or he.

Mozilla

I currently work for the design system behind Mozilla's Firefox.

I support our team by documenting and maintaining the browser's user interface and experience, while promoting our software design best practices.

Sprout Social

I brought consistency across Sprout Social's social media publishing products by focusing on contributions to Sprout's new design system called Seeds. Seeds runs on the modular user interface frameworks React and Figma. I developed and documented reusable components and design patterns into Figma libraries, Storybook for React, and public-facing docs run on Gatsby.

dscout

I lead the design and redesign of several dscout features during a major enhancement of the codebase. In order to improve data handling, the engineering team overhauled the platform using the performant tools Elixir, GraphQL, and React. I established a framework for UI kits and style guides in Sketch and implemented reusable components and design patterns in the Storybook for React.
