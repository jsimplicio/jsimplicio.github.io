Hey, I'm Jules. I design and code.

I am designing an app for people who want quick access to their design system's tokens, and building it with SwiftUI.

At work, I design and develop tools for social media managers.

I help software teams adhere to UX patterns, and often help with the creation and promotion of new ones too. I also help introduce and promote consistency in our product by being a core member of our UI Kit team.

I am a design systems advocate. I educate others on working with design systems and I promote org-wide systems adoption.

I am systems thinker. I think about the role of systems, how they work, evolve, and how outside forces affect them.
