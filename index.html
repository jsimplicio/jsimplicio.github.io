Jules Simplicio designs and develops software. They are currently helping build tools and product experiences at Sprout Social.

Product Strategy

I help businesses stand out by focusing on a vision and defining goals. I help build a roadmap so products can achieve set goals in order to provide value to customers, and I can measure their success.

Wireframing and Prototyping

Based on strategic goals, I work on wireframes and use them to prototype user needs that are used for user testing in order to validate any assumptions.

User Experience Research

I improve products usability and usefulness by empathizing with customers, and by understanding their behaviors. I use a variety of methods to gather qualitative and quantitative data and insights.

Web and Desktop App Design

I design the interface and experience of products. I create innovative and improved experiences with outstanding functionality and usability. I componetize designs to help build and maintain design systems.

Front-End Software Development

I prototype designs in code during ideating and testing. I translate final designs into production code with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I use modern technologies and frameworks including Scss, and React. I help develop components and document design systems.

Mobile App Design

I design mobile experiences and interfaces for native Android and iOS, and React Native apps.
