About

I grew up with a computer in my room, and was thankful that I learned to code and design websites at a very young age. I was surfing the web during the dot-com bubble while starting to experiment with hypertext.

I ended up becoming a software product designer and front-end engineer designer. I have over 6 years of experience that were spent researching, designing, and developing software products used by hundreds of thousands of people.

I am currently building social media management software products at Sprout Social, serving and connecting people with powerful social media tools.

In the past, I have:

  • Designed and developed research products at dscout.
  • Designed e-commerce experiences for Threadless and their service Artist Shops.
  • Designed for Basecampers, and for The Distance at Basecamp.

I enjoy participating in dialogues about design. Panel appearances: Tune-up: Portfolio Polish and Design Pride: Insights and Experiences.
